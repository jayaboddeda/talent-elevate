Quick Answers

Things candidates usually ask

Is there any charge to register?

No. Posting your resume with Talent Elevate is completely free for candidates. We're paid by the hiring company once you're successfully placed.

Will my current employer find out I'm exploring?

No. We never share your profile with your current employer or with companies you flag. You can also tell us specific organisations to exclude.

How fast will I hear back?

You'll hear from us within 48 working hours, either with a matching role or a clear note that we're holding your profile until something fits.

What file formats can I upload?

We accept .doc, .docx, .rtf, and .pdf files up to 2 MB. PDF is preferred for consistent formatting on the recruiter's side.

Can I update my profile later?

Yes, just submit again or email your updated CV to hello@talentelevate.in with your name in the subject line.

Prefer to email your resume?

Send your CV with a one-line note about the kind of role you're after, and we'll take it from there.
