Engagement Models

Three ways we plug into your project

Onsite Deployment

Resources work from your office under your supervision. Ideal for tight collaboration, sensitive systems, and integrated delivery teams.

Offsite / Remote Pods

Dedicated pods working from our facility or distributed locations, with full output ownership and reduced infrastructure overhead.

Hybrid Squads

Mix-and-match models with senior leads onsite and execution teams remote, tuned to your project rhythm and budget.
