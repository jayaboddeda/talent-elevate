01
Direct Search

Head Hunting

Best For
Mid-to-senior, niche, leadership
Typical Cycle
3 - 8 weeks

Talent Elevate runs core headhunting by combining its internal database with reference generation built from each client's specific need. Our consultants understand the brief deeply, then map the relevant industry, identify potential candidates, build interest in the role, and recommend a tightly curated shortlist through a pre-defined process.

By tailoring the search to your unique requirement, we shortlist the individual or team most capable of taking on what's ahead, not just resumes that match keywords.

03

04

Closure

Interview coordination, offer negotiation, joining, post-join check-in.

We arrange walk-in interviews for entry-level positions at a venue decided by the client. Talent Elevate is responsible for driving traffic to the venue, running promotional activities to ensure strong, qualified footfall on drive day, and managing the floor end-to-end.

Posters, social campaigns, SMS, college networks, and database alerts all get used in coordination so you don't end up with an empty hall, or worse, a full hall of mismatched profiles.

Outreach Hiring

Advertisements & Response Handling

Best For
Branded launches, multi-role drives
Typical Cycle
2 - 5 weeks

Talent Elevate advertises on the client's behalf and runs the entire process, including initial interviews. Or, if the client has already advertised, the inbound application screening can be outsourced to us so the inbox doesn't become the bottleneck.

We design the ad, pick the right channels (job boards, social, print where it still makes sense), handle the response volume, screen against the JD, and present a calibrated shortlist with the panel-ready notes.

Talent Elevate reaches out to a large number of Engineering, MBA, and MCA campuses across the country to facilitate campus hiring for its clients. The final campus list is mutually decided between Talent Elevate and the client.

We provide for all the general facilities at the venue and ensure smooth functioning of the process, from coordination with placement officers to logistics, scheduling, and on-the-ground support on drive day.
