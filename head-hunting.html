Service Overview

A firm grip on core headhunting

Talent Elevate has a firm grip on core headhunting, employing a deep internal database and generating references as per each client's requirement. Our consultants are trained to understand client needs and undertake extensive research, mapping the relevant industry, identifying potential job seekers, and building genuine interest in your company.

We recommend only interested, suitable profiles, through a set, pre-defined process. By tailoring our search to the unique needs of our clients, we shortlist the individual or team most capable of handling the challenges that lie ahead.

Mandate Types

Three ways we run a search

Retained Executive Search

Dedicated, exclusive search for CXO and senior-leadership mandates. Deep mapping, weekly reviews, guaranteed effort.

Confidential Search

Discreet hiring for sensitive roles, replacements, or stealth-mode initiatives. Your name stays under wraps until it shouldn't.

Niche Specialist Search

Hard-to-find skill sets, emerging tech, regulated functions. Targeted research where job-board sourcing falls flat.

How It Works

From brief to signed offer

A transparent, four-stage engagement, with timelines and check-ins agreed up front.

01

Discover

Role brief, success profile, must-haves, deal-breakers, and culture cues.

02

Map & Identify

Industry mapping, target list, longlist of named candidates, calibration with you.

03

Engage & Assess

Discreet outreach, deep evaluation, references, and tailored shortlist for your interviews.

04

Close

Negotiation support, offer management, notice-period coaching, and 90-day follow-through.
